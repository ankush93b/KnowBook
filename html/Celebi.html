Celebi

Celebi is a Turkey based company which provides airport ground handling services and cargo handling. It is operating as 'Celebi Airport Services India' in India and providing services in New Delhi, Kerala, Bengaluru, Hyderabad and Goa.

Ingoing Events:

May 17, 2025

Celebi has launched a legal challenge in Delhi High Court to Indian govt's decision to overturn its security clearance, arguing that "vague" national security concerns were cited without reasoning and arguing it would impact 3,791 jobs and investor confidence, and was issued without any warning to the company. The Indian government revoked Celebi's security clearance in the "interest of national security."

"Mere rhetoric of national security without elaborating upon in what manner is an entity a threat to national security is unsustainable in law," the company said in the filing. The order "fails to disclose any specific or substantive reason except for a vague and general reference to 'national security' provides no reasons or justification."

In its filing, Celebi said that while its shareholders were registered in Turkey, "majority end control" of the group is held by companies that do not have Turkish incorporation or origin.
