Vaibhav Taneja

In-News

Taneja has garnered significant attention when his compensation details were revealed in May 2025. Taneja received a whopping $139 million in 2024, which notably exceeds the pay of top tech leaders such as Microsoft's Satya Nadella at $79.1 million and Google's Sundar Pichai at $10.73 million in 2024. This substantial sum encompasses various forms of compensation beyond direct salary. Taneja's basic salary at Tesla was $400,000 (Rs 3.33 crore). The remainder consisted of stock options and equity linked to performance metrics.
