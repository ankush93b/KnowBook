NIBE Limited

NIBE Limited is an Indian defence technology company engaged in the development, manufacturing, and integration of defence systems based in Pune, Maharashtra, India.

Products

The company has two divisions: ‘Defence Structure’, which focuses on manufacturing mobile weapon systems, rocket launchers, modular bridges, and other components of defense equipment; and ‘Defence Electronics’, which specializes in embedded systems such as rugged dual-display consoles, network switches, Ethernet switches, and various related products.
