Emirates Draw

Emirates Draw is a global online lottery draw, operated and managed by Tychero with prizes up to AED 100 million.

The Emirates Draw had paused its UAE operations at the end of 2023 following new regulations introduced by the UAE’s Commercial Gaming Regulatory Authority (GCGRA).

Winners

In May 2025, Sriram Rajagopalan, a retired Indian engineer from Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India bagged one of the largest lottery prizes, bagging ₹225 crore (approx. $27 million, Dh100 million) in the Emirates Draw MEGA7 game.
