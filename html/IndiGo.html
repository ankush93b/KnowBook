IndiGo

Averted disasters

On May 21, 2025, an IndiGo Airlines flight 6E2142 carrying 227 passengers from Delhi to Srinagar encountered severe turbulence when a sudden hailstorm struck near its destination causing panic among those onboard. The pilot declared an emergency and successfully landed the aircraft at the Srinagar International Airport and no injuries were reported. Though the aircraft suffered visible damage to the nose due to the mid-air turbulence and was declared 'Aircraft on Ground' for urgent repairs. The passengers described it as a near-death experience.
